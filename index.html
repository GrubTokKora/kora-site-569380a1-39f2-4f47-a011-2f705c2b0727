About 914 Pop Ups

Curating Unforgettable Local Experiences

Explore the best of Westchester and its neighboring cities with Melissa Tomlin of 914PopUps! From exclusive pop-up markets and artisan fairs to exciting community festivals and boutique events, there's always something happening near you.

Melissa curates unique experiences that bring together local creators, small businesses, and residents for unforgettable moments. Every event is thoughtfully designed to showcase the incredible talent within our community while creating spaces for genuine connection.
